BEST PRECAUTIONS

Best Precautions:

Here are some of the best precautions which will make your tech life secure:


• Never open a file/link which is sent by a stranger.

• Never open a suspicious file/link.

• Never download an application from a third party website.

• Never open a shortened URL.

• Create a "Passphrase (12 - 15 letters)" not a "Password (06 - 12 letters)". 
• Use VPN + HTTPS Everywhere. 

• Always verify an email before replying or clicking on it.

• Never reply or click on a link, which is sent by a stranger.

• Check the authenticity of pop up before clicking on it.

• Check the URL of the website before entering something on it.

• Use end-to-end encrypted services.

• Never share your personal details on call/email.

• Never share your OTP.

• Do not open a file/link/app sent by a stranger.

• Never use an external device given by a stranger.

• Use an antivirus.

• Beware of fake websites.
