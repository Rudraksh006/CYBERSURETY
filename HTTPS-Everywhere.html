What Is HTTPS Everywhere?

HTTPS Everywhere is a browser extension, that forces HTTPS on every website, even on those websites who don’t use HTTPS, and using HTTPS is way more secure than HTTP. 

Main Benefit Of HTTPS Everywhere:

Most of the network/WiFi attacks are associated with MITM (Man In The Middle) attacks, in MITM attack the attacker can downgrade any HTTPS website to HTTP, and once the is in HTTP, he will be able see all your data associated with the website, for instance, if you try go to https://facebook.com/ , the attacker will first downgrade the website to http://facebook.com/ , and after downgrading the website, the attacker will be able to see all the activities that you do on Facebook including your login id, login password, etc.

Installation:

You can install it on most of the common browsers like Mozilla Firefox, Opera & Google Chrome.   

Download Links (Desktop) : 
        • Google Chrome - https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/https-everywhere/gcbommkclmclpchllfjekcdonpmejbdp         
        • Mozilla Firefox - https://www.eff.org/files/https-everywhere-latest.xpi         
        • Microsoft Edge - https://microsoftedge.microsoft.com/addons/detail/https-everywhere/fchjpkplmbeeeaaogdbhjbgbknjobohb·              • Opera - https://addons.opera.com/en/extensions/details/https-everywhere/ 
        • Brave – Included.        
        • TOR Browser – Included.    

Download Links (Mobile) :    
        • Mozilla Firefox - https://www.eff.org/files/https-everywhere-latest.xpi 
        • Google Chrome - https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/https-everywhere/gcbommkclmclpchllfjekcdonpmejbdp 
        • Brave – Included.         
        • TOR Browser – Included.        
        • Onion Browser – Included.    

After going to the download webpage, add the extension in your browser.  

It may ask you for a confirmation, click on the “Add extension” button to confirm.  

Click on the “Extensions” icon present beside the URL/Address bar.  

After opening the “Extensions” tab, pin HTTPS Everywhere.  

Done! Now HTTPS Everywhere has been installed successfully & we are ready to use it!

Turning On/Off HTTPS Everywhere:

Turning On HTTPS Everywhere:

Open the HTTPS Everywhere extension, you will see a message saying “HTTPS Everywhere is OFF”.  

Click on the switch present beside the message saying “HTTPS Everywhere is OFF”. 

Now the message will change to “HTTPS Everywhere is ON”.  

Turning Off HTTPS Everywhere:

Open the HTTPS Everywhere extension, you will see a message saying “HTTPS Everywhere is ON”.  

Click on the switch present beside the message saying “HTTPS Everywhere is ON”. 

Now the message will change to “HTTPS Everywhere is OFF”.

Turning On/Off “Encrypt All Sites”:

Turning On “Encrypt All Sites”:

Open the HTTPS Everywhere extension, you will see a message saying “Encrypt All Sites Eligible is OFF”.  

Click on the switch present beside the message saying “Encrypt All Sites Eligible is OFF”. 

Now the message will change to “HTTPS Everywhere is ON”.  

Turning Off “Encrypt All Sites”:

Open the HTTPS Everywhere extension, you will see a message saying “Encrypt All Sites Eligible is ON”.  

Click on the switch present beside the message saying “Encrypt All Sites Eligible is ON”. 

Now the message will change to “HTTPS Everywhere is OFF”.

Rules, About HTTPS Everywhere & Donate To EFF:

“Rules, About HTTPS Everywhere & Donate To EFF” are the 3 buttons present at the bottom of the extension.  

View All Rules : This button will redirect you to the webpage of HTTPS Everywhere containing all the rules of HTTPS Everywhere.   

About HTTPS Everywhere : This button will redirect you to the webpage of HTTPS Everywhere which will give you a detailed description of HTTPS Everywhere.   

Donate to EFF: This button will redirect you to the webpage of EFF where you can donate money.

How To Reset Settings To Default? 

To reset settings to default, open the HTTPS Everywhere extension & click on the “Reset to Defaults” button.

How To Disable HTTPS Everywhere On A Particular Website?

Go to the website on which you want to disable HTTPS Everywhere.   
Open the HTTPS Everywhere extension & then click on the “Disable HTTPS Everywhere on this site” button, and then reload the website.
